Marathon Ape Run
Virtual pledges for charity - make runners earn it!

How many pledges can I place?

There's no limit on how many pledges you can make or on the total value. The more pledges, the more my motivation to finish the race!

When is it?

Dublin City Marathon takes place on Sunday October 30th. You can place pledges any time from now and during the race.

Where will my money go?

Payments will be sent (when you pay yourself!) to Children's Medical and Research Foundation in Crumlin Hospital.

Do I pay before or after the event?

After, once you see how many of your pledges I collect. Of course, if you're feeling generous, we won't stop you donating everything!

What happens if I don't pay what you've collected?

Nothing at all; this is all honour-based. Of course, if you've gotten this far, we know you're a nice person!

How can we see where you are?

I have smartphones that use GPS and relay my positions to the aperun servers. You'll be able to follow me on the map during training and the marathon itself.

How does all this sorcery work? Are you from the future?

Goodness, no! We just the made the most of some very clever technology, including JavaScript, geo-location browser extensions, jQuery, Google Maps, Google App Engine and Android (lots of Android...).

Are my credit card details safe?

We don't handle the credit card transaction here; we'll just send you to the charity's secure payment site when it's time to make the payment.

What's all this about maps and apes and other mumbo jumbo? Can't I just donate some money?

We hope you'll enjoy the fun of the event, but we understand if you're busy! Straight donations to our charity are, of course, very welcome.

What do I do if I have a problem with the site?

Feedback is good - drop me a line on apeworks on Facebook, @seanmulvany on twitter or an email at sean@apeworks.com.
